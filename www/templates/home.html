Welcome to Tribe!

Tribe is a crowd-sensing mobile app.

We're interested in seeing the trends in Vancouverites moods & their responses to questions of the day.

In a future release, we will incorporate the Tribe component to analyze responses across different user groups.

Note: with each response, we also collect the devices UUIDs.

To use

1. Report your mood as often as you like
2. Answer the Question of the Day
3. Schedule a daily reminder in the Settings page
